About BlockVote

Secure Voting

Cryptographically secure voting ensures that your vote cannot be tampered with once cast.

Full Transparency

All votes are recorded on the blockchain and can be verified by anyone without revealing voter identity.

Decentralized

No central authority controls the voting process, eliminating the possibility of manipulation.

Instant Results

Results are calculated in real-time as votes are cast, with final tallying done automatically.
